## Garden
Large garden with a [cistern](http://vocab.getty.edu/page/aat/300052558), water basin, and irrigation channel.

### Garden Description
A large garden area extended across the rear of this house (to the south). A [cistern](http://vocab.getty.edu/page/aat/300052558) opening was located in the rear wall of the house and a basin built against the west wall had an irrigation channel to the garden. Another water basin was located near the east wall. Additional water was admitted to the rear part of the garden by an opening under the east end of the north wall from the garden to the back of entrance no. 11. Stairs leading to an upper floor were located in the garden.
